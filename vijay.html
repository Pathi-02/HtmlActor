Vijay

Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is a highly popular Indian actor primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Vijay is often referred to by his fans as "Thalapathy" (commander). He is one of the most celebrated and influential actors in the Tamil film industry, with a massive fan base across India and among Tamil-speaking audiences worldwide
